Nomination Process

Step 1: Fill out the nomination form with the nominee’s details, including name, email, company, designation, gender, award category, and reason for nomination.

Step 2: Submit the completed form. You will receive a confirmation email with the details of your nomination.

Step 3: Our selection committee will review all nominations and shortlist candidates based on the provided information.

Step 4: Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for further details and an interview if necessary.

Step 5: Winners will be announced at the Corporate Excellence Awards ceremony. All nominees will be notified of the results.

Thank you for participating and recognizing excellence in the corporate world!
